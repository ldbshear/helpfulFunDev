Like most people, Covid disrupted my family's way of life. I went from interviewing for in-person software developer jobs, to teaching my elementary age kids. Different personalities, different sensory needs, different temperaments, different strengths and weaknesses. Disaster right? No.

In an environment of accountability, flexibilty, and various learning supports, they've gained confidence in their ability to learn. As a mother, I'm grateful for online applications that present or reinforce learning concepts. And as a developer, it excites me to code something that could help someone, gain confidence in their abilities.
